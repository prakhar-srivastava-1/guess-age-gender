Welcome to Guessify!

Guessify is a fun little application which predicts your age, gender and nationality based on your name. To give it a go, all you have to do is,

  • Open the website
  • Type the URL - https://localhost:5000/guess/your_name
  • Hit Enter

You should then see the predictions made by Guessify.
